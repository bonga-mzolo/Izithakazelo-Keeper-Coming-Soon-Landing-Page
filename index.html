What are Izithakazelo?

More than just a surname, Izithakazelo are the poetic heartbeat of our identity. They are clan based praise names used by Nguni speaking peoples including the Zulu, Xhosa, Swazi, and Ndebele to invoke the spirit and history of their lineage.
