About Izithakazelo Keeper

Our Mission

Izithakazelo is a digital initiative dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Zulu clans. We aim to create a comprehensive, accurate, and accessible database of izithakazelo (clan praises) for current and future generations.

What are Izithakazelo?

Izithakazelo are traditional Zulu clan praises that identify a person's lineage and heritage. They serve as:

  • Cultural Identity: Connecting individuals to their ancestors and clan history
  • Oral History: Preserving stories, achievements, and characteristics of clans
  • Social Connection: Creating bonds between people of the same clan
  • Traditional Protocol: Used in ceremonies, greetings, and important occasions
